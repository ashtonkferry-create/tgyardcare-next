Workflow flow:

1. Schedule Trigger (id: tg144-trigger, name: "Daily 6 AM Crew Field Reminders")
   - type: n8n-nodes-base.scheduleTrigger, typeVersion: 1.2
   - rule: every day at 6:00 AM CT (11:00 UTC)
   - Early enough for crew to see before heading out

2. Fetch Today's Jobs (id: tg144-jobs, name: "Fetch Today's Scheduled Jobs")
   - type: n8n-nodes-base.httpRequest, typeVersion: 4.2
   - GET jobber_email_events where event_type=job_scheduled and parsed_scheduled_date=today
   - continueOnFail: true

3. Build Field Reminders (id: tg144-build, name: "Build Crew Field Marketing Reminders")
   - type: n8n-nodes-base.code, typeVersion: 2
   - For each job, create a reminder message:
     "FIELD MARKETING CHECKLIST - {customer_name}
      {address if available}
      Service: {service_type}
      Time: {scheduled_time if available}

      Before leaving the job:
      [ ] Place yard sign in visible front area
      [ ] Leave 3 door hangers on neighboring doors
      [ ] Carry business cards in truck
      [ ] Take a quick before/after photo

      Reply 'sign {job_id}' when yard sign is placed
      Reply 'photo {job_id}' to attach before/after photo"

4. Send Crew Reminders (id: tg144-send, name: "Send Crew Reminders to Telegram")
   - type: n8n-nodes-base.telegram, typeVersion: 1.2
   - credentials: V404qLIDXjmyzNeS
   - chatId: OWNER_TELEGRAM_CHAT_ID
   - Send one combined message with all jobs listed
   - Or individual messages per job (better for tracking replies)

5. Listen for Sign Replies (id: tg144-webhook, name: "Yard Sign Placement Webhook")
   - Use Telegram trigger (separate webhook path: tg144-sign-tracking)
   - Parse "sign {job_id}" replies
   - When received:
     - POST yard_sign_placements to Supabase: job_id, location (from job data), placed_at, status: "active"
     - Reply: "Yard sign logged for {customer_name}. Will remind to collect in 7 days."

6. Yard Sign Collection Reminder (id: tg144-collect, name: "Check for Sign Collection Due")
   - type: n8n-nodes-base.code, typeVersion: 2
   - Check yard_sign_placements where placed_at < 7 days ago and status = "active"
   - For each, include in daily reminder: "COLLECT SIGN: {location} (placed {days} ago)"
   - After 14 days, auto-update status to "expired" (assumed collected or lost)
